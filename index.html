Our Philosophy

Your body evolved over millions of years to extract nutrients from whole foods. Every vitamin and mineral comes packaged with co-factors, enzymes, and compounds that make absorption more efficient. Sahan Vita teaches you to nourish yourself the way nature always intended.

Did You Know?

Vitamin C increases iron absorption by up to 3x when eaten together. Vitamin D is technically a hormone — your skin makes it from sunlight. Magnesium is involved in over 300 enzyme reactions in your body.
